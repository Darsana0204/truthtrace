# TruthTrace

## AI-Powered Digital Asset Protection System

TruthTrace is an AI-powered platform that detects duplicate and near-duplicate media, verifies originality using digital fingerprinting, predicts copyright misuse risk, and generates ownership certificates to protect digital content from unauthorized reuse.

---

## Problem Statement

Sports organizations and media companies create valuable digital assets such as match photos, exclusive videos, promotional content, and event coverage. Once uploaded online, this content is often copied, reposted, edited, and redistributed without permission.

This leads to:

* Copyright infringement
* Unauthorized content reuse
* Fake ownership claims
* Brand reputation damage
* Revenue loss from piracy

Most existing systems detect issues only after the damage is done.

---

## Our Solution

TruthTrace helps organizations proactively protect digital assets by:

* Detecting exact duplicate uploads
* Identifying near-duplicate modified content
* Generating a TruthScore for originality
* Predicting copyright misuse risk
* Creating ownership certificates as proof of originality
* Providing Gemini AI-powered explanations and admin recommendations

---

## Key Features

### Exact Duplicate Detection

Uses SHA-256 digital fingerprinting to detect exact duplicate files.

### Near Duplicate Detection

Detects modified versions such as cropped, resized, watermarked, or slightly edited media.

### TruthScore System

Assigns a score from 0–100 to measure originality confidence.

### AI Risk Prediction

Classifies uploads into Low, Medium, or High copyright risk.

### Gemini AI Explanation

Uses Google Gemini AI to generate professional analysis and recommendations.

### Ownership Certificate

Generates a PDF certificate for verified unique content.
